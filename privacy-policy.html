Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 5, 2026

Privacy-first architecture: Image processing happens entirely on your device. No image data is ever transmitted to or stored on any server — this is a technical property of the service, not merely a policy commitment.

This Privacy Policy explains how ImageToolSuite collects, uses and protects personal data in accordance with the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and applicable Austrian law. We take privacy seriously and have designed this service to process as little personal data as technically possible.

2. How This Tool Works – Local Processing

Images are processed exclusively and entirely within the user's browser. No image data is uploaded to any server at any time.

When you use any tool on ImageToolSuite — including the image checker, resize tool, compression tool, DPI checker or any other image processing feature — your image files are loaded directly into your browser's memory using standard browser APIs (HTML5 File API and Canvas API). All analysis, processing and transformation happens locally on your device.

Your images never leave your device. They are not transmitted to our servers, not stored on any server, not logged and not accessible to us in any form. This is a fundamental technical property of the service, not merely a policy commitment.

Processed or downloaded output files are generated in your browser and delivered directly to your device via the browser's native download mechanism — again without any server involvement.

When you visit this website, standard technical data is automatically recorded in server log files, including IP address, browser type, operating system, referrer URL and time of access. See Section 4a for full details.

We do not store contact data beyond the period necessary to handle your inquiry, unless legal obligations require longer retention.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data only for as long as necessary for the purposes for which it was collected:

  • Server logs: Retained only for as long as necessary for security, operational and troubleshooting purposes and automatically deleted thereafter.
  • Email correspondence: Retained only for the duration necessary to handle your inquiry, typically no longer than 6 months, unless legal obligations require longer retention.
  • Image data: Not retained at all — images never leave your browser and are never stored on any server.

9. Security

This website is served exclusively over HTTPS (TLS encryption), which protects data in transit between your browser and our infrastructure. Cloudflare's network provides additional DDoS protection and security hardening at the infrastructure level.

Since image files are never transmitted to or stored on any server, there is no risk of image data being exposed through a server-side security incident. The local processing architecture is itself the primary security measure for your image data.

10. Automated Decision-Making and Profiling

No automated decision-making or profiling takes place on this website. We do not use personal data to build user profiles, make automated decisions with legal or similarly significant effects, or infer characteristics about individual users.
